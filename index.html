The Evolution of AI

1950 The Introduction of the Concept of Artifical Intelligence Alan Turning began exploring the concept of a machine coded to perform a task. Turning lacked the technology needed to prove this theory.
1956 First Study Was Conducted John McCarthy a professor at Dartmouth and a group of researchers began exploring the concept of thinking machines.
1960-1970 Robots The first chatbot, Eliza, was created in 1966. Eliza was able to answer questions. The first robot, Shakey the Robot, was created in 1972. Shakey the Robot used a camera to control the enviorment and perform tasks.
1980 Unity The first conference of artifical intelligence was held in 1977. Then, the American Assoication of Artifical Intelligence was established in 1980.
1986 AI in Automation Ernst Dickmanns invented the first self driving car in Germmany.
2011 Apple use of AI Apple's first utilizaiton of artifical intelligence was through an Iphone feature in 2011. The feature that was created was named Siri. Siri is a virtual assistant that can perform tasks or answer questions. Apple then released its own form of artifical intelligence and named it Apple Intelligence in 2024.
2014 Amazon & AI Amazon invented Alexa a virtual assistant that answers questions at a limit.
2020 ChatGPT ChatGPT an advanced chatbot was created with the ability to generate images and texts.
